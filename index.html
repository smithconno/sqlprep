SQL Inner Join is a keyword that selects records that have matching values in both tables. To help make sense of this command, just think of the middle of a venn diagram. The INNER JOIN command comes directly after the FROM command, and you can use inner join with 3 tables, you just use a seperate inner join command after the first one. This type of join matches rows from the participating tables based on a common column.

In the dictionary, a data scientist is a person employed to analyze and interpret complex digital data, such as the usage statistics of a website, especially in order to assist a business in its decision-making. A data scientist must be an expert in both programming, statistics, calculus, and linear algebra among other things. Data scientists create various machine based learning tools. If you think of computer science as a tree, data science is a brach of the tree. Computer science mainly deals with a focus on software engineering and development, whereas data science uses those algorithms to extract knowledge from data.
